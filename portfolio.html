Portfolio Management

Meet our platform LISA

Lynxcap Investment Software Application or LISA for short is our proprietary platform that allows us to achieve operational and cost efficiency.
Accessed from anywhere at anytime
In addition to a number of tools developed and hosted internally, LynxCap utilizes third party cloud solutions for reporting and ticketing. Using secured connections, these can be accessed from anywhere at anytime which also allows us to focus on our own strengths for internal applications.
Link between certified partners and legal offices
LISA's reporting and analytical capabilities allows us to be up to date in relation to over 30,000 claims and REO's we have acquired. LISA's systems architecture creates a link between certified collection partners and legal offices working in the workout on the acquired portfolios and our team.
Viewing and analyzing data
By harnessing the power of data we ensure that our investments are backed by thorough analysis and insights driving growth and value in every acquisition we undertake.

Core reports

  • Collection report
    Comparison of collections vs forecast on portfolio level, payer analysis and investment return analysis
  • Asset Base
    Overview of assets in ownership resp. claim collaterals, info on valuations, upcoming and past auctions, sales
  • Case Overview
    Full overview of selected claims, borrowers or borrower groups including collections, forecasts, dynamic core data, etc
  • Pipeline
    Overview of the current LynxCap investment pipeline
