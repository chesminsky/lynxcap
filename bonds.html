Why Luxembourg?

Renowned for its robust regulatory framework and international reach the Luxembourg Stock Exchange (LSE) adheres to world class standards including audit requirements and offers unparalleled visibility and credibility to our offerings. Luxembourg's strategic location at the hear of Europe, combined with its reputation as a leading financial center ensures that our bonds attract a diverse and global investor base.

The exchanges advanced trading infrastructure and commitment to transparency make it an ideal venue for our bonds. By listing our bonds on the EMTF section of the LSE reinforces our dedication to maintaining the highest standards of governance and compliance
